Why Our Murrel is Different
Murrel (Viral Meen — விரால் மீன்) is revered in Tamil Nadu for its near-boneless, protein-rich flesh. Raised in zero-chemical, farm-integrated ponds — fed entirely by natural organic matter. No pellets, no hormones, no stress.
